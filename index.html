About us

30 years ago we started our first record label Lush Recordings with the Truelove Label Collective in London. 

We released Ambient, Trance, Techno and Experimental Breakbeat from such artists as Friends, Lovers & Family, Quietman, Mr Oz and Larry Lush to name just a few. 

We never stopped making music but we did stop making records.

Now we are back!

Meet the Artists

Larry Lush 

Larry Lush (Laurence Elliott-Potter) has been producing and creating experimental and intriguing electronic music since the early '90's. From Ambient to uplifting melodic trance and everything in-between. He has worked and written with the likes of Tall Paul, Fergie, Mix Master Morris and Coldcut  to name a just a few. Now back in the studio producing  a number of coproductions and solo works and running a new label.

Can you guess which one?

Friends Lovers & Family

30 YEARS AGO - THE ORIGINAL LONDON BASED ART-HOUSE TECHNO BREAK BEAT TRIO. 

Formed with Wilfisms and Mk Ultra and originally signed to Rising-High Records you can find their back catalogue here and stay tuned for a new Box-Set release coming soon. 

They recently appeared on the FABRICLIVE 100: Kode9 & Burial Album available here.

Quietman

Quietman formed by William Frost and Larry Lush in the Uk in 90's and originally on Platipus Recordings now back in full force.

Back in the studio working on a new album for Black Unicorn Recordings and playing at a festival near you.

L.MO

L.MO.  grew up in a musical family.

Inspired by his good
friend Zolex L.MO began his career in 2008 exploring minimal techno and tech house playing at private events in Belgium.

Hi is in his element playing funky, groovy house, afro and tribal house, melodic techno, deep bass techno and psytechno.

In 2024 L.MO  expanded his skills to producing his own electronic music.
